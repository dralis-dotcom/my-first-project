DESCRIPTION (English — ~200 words)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MemoryMaster is the ultimate daily training companion for anyone serious about
developing a world-class memory. Built around the proven techniques of memory
champions — including Dominic O'Brien's Dominic System and Tony Buzan's Mind
Mapping — it gives you a structured gym for your brain, right in your pocket.

Train across seven classic memory-sport disciplines every day:
• Numbers — encode random digit strings using Dominic pairs
• Cards — memorise a shuffled deck at competition speed
• Words — recall lists of random concrete nouns in order
• Binary — blaze through 0/1 sequences
• Names & Faces — pair names to faces like a networking pro
• Images — remember and replay procedurally generated abstract images
• Historic Dates — lock world events to their exact years

Each session is timed, scored, and tracked. A built-in spaced-repetition
flashcard engine (SM-2) keeps your Dominic table sharp, and one-tap AI
mnemonics (Claude + DALL-E) invent vivid images on demand.

Streak tracking, a home-screen widget, and optional iCloud sync across all
your devices keep you accountable day after day.

No subscription. No ads. Just pure memory training.

PRIVACY — DATA COLLECTION
  MemoryMaster does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • All user-generated content (Dominic table, decks, mind maps, session
    results) is stored exclusively on-device in the iOS app sandbox.
  • Streak data can optionally be synced via iCloud Key-Value Storage
    (user's own iCloud account; Apple handles storage).
  • API keys (Anthropic, OpenAI) are stored in the iOS Keychain and are
    transmitted only to the respective third-party API.
  • No analytics, no advertising identifiers, no crash reporters.
